Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe is one of the quickest ways to make sure your family eats good for the night. By following this recipe you will learn how to please the entire family

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Step by Step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
